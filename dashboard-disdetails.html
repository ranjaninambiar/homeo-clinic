SIMPLE NON-TOXIC GOITRE
Any non-malignant enlargement of the thyroid gland, unassociated with the hyper-secretion of thyroid hormones constitutes a simple non-toxic goiter. Treatment: Thyroidinum – when simple goiter is soft. Iodine – In dark complexioned subjects with dark eyes, who have emaciated look. Calcarea Carb – In pale subjects of soft fibre with cold moist feet. Fluoric acid – If all the above remedies fail. Spongia – In old hard goiters.
